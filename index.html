La Decima

92:48. The minute every Madridista shall live to remember. The minute that deliverd the long-awaited La Decima.

Luka Modric – the little magician – whips a corner kick to the edge of the six-yard box, Sergio Ramos, El-capitan meets the ball with a thumping header,the ball nestles in the buttom right corner,evading Courtois' desparing dive. Real Madrid have tied the game after trailing Athletico Madrid for more than three quaters of the match. The score reads 1-1.

The 2014 UEFA Champions League goes to extra time. There's pandemonium in the stands. Enroute to the final, Real madrid had beaten:

  • Bayern Munich
  • Schalke 04
  • Borussia Dortmund

Real Madrid schooled German football in 2014.
